CRM Sales Performance Dashboard

Developed an interactive CRM Sales Performance Dashboard in Looker Studio to visualize and analyze sales trends over time. The dashboard provided insights into total sales, average sales value, sales orders, and performance by product, sector, and sales manager. Key metrics such as average days to close, orders won, and sector-wise sales distribution were highlighted to optimize sales strategies and improve decision-making for different product lines and managers. The dashboard facilitated tracking sales growth, identifying high-performing sectors, and evaluating manager efficiency.
